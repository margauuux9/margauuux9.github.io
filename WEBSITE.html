ABOUT THIS ISLAND :

Reunion Island is an intense island and a land of contrasts and diversity. Located 9,345 km from Paris, in the middle of the southern hemisphere. Reunion Island is multi-ethnic, authentic with its cultural diversity. Reunion Island is a tropical destination with breathtaking landscapes

ABOUT THIS CULTURE

It is a real rainbow of populations, religion, culture. Cultures coexist on this land which brings to the island a cultural and religious richness. This mixture of populations has given Reunion Island a diversity of religions.

ABOUT THIS LANDSCAPES

Reunion Island is 255 times smaller than France and yet abounds in diverse landscapes.
You can see its peaks like the Piton des Neiges, or the Piton de la Fournaise which is its volcano. There are also its circuses, Cilaos, Salazie and Mafate and its high ramparts which are listed as UNESCO World Heritage Sites.
The Piton de la Fournaise is one of the most striking places on the island. In a few kilometers the green tropical landscape becomes a mineral and lunar desert. One or two spectacular eruptions can be observed per year.

About of Gastronomy

Reunion Island is an island with specialities to turn your head. It is an exotic cocktail of a thousand colours, scents and flavours. In the Reunionese culinary tradition, the aperitif is important. Samoussas, corks and chilli candies are served.


Chez Loulou is a must for eating Reunionese specialities.
For dishes generally based on fish, pork, or poultry, they are served with rice, grains, breds, achards, or tomato rougail. All this leaves the emphasis on spices
